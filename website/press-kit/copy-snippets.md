## Five-hundred-character press paragraph

> Murmur is a local-first dictation utility for macOS. You hold the fn key, speak, release — and cleaned text drops into whichever app held the cursor. The transcription runs entirely on your Mac, via whisper.cpp, accelerated on the Apple GPU. No socket opens during a dictation, no account is required, no telemetry is collected. Four cleanup profiles (Raw, Casual, Formal, Code) apply deterministic rules — there is no second model rewriting what you said. MIT-licensed. Free, forever.

(485 characters.)
