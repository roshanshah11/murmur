How it works

  1. Hold fn

    Double-tap and hold. The notch turns red.

  2. Speak

    Whisper.cpp transcribes locally on the GPU.

  3. Paste

    Text drops into whatever app you were in.

Local-first, by construction.

  • Zero network. No socket opens during transcription. Verify with Little Snitch.
  • No telemetry. No analytics, crash reports, or anonymous pings. Period.
  • MIT-licensed. Read it, fork it, ship your own. github.com/roshanshah11/murmur.

Built like a real Mac app.
